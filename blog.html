Catching Moles: Cybersecurity for Digital Nomads

Welcome to my blog! In a world where freedom to work from anywhere is the ultimate lifestyle, cybersecurity is often overlooked, leaving many digital nomads vulnerable. Here, I bridge the gap between adventure and security, offering practical guidance to help you travel safely and protect your work on the go. Whether you're navigating open Wi-Fi networks in a café in Bali, managing client data from a coworking space in Lisbon, or simply want peace of mind on your next adventure, you’ll find insights here on the best practices, tools, and strategies to keep your digital life secure. Join me in exploring the tools and tips that allow you to stay connected safely, no matter where the road takes you.
